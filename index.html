Bolivia

Cerro Rico, also known as the Rich Mountain, is a legendary silver mountain located in Potosi, Bolivia. During the Spanish colonial period, it was the world's largest source of silver. The mountain stands at 4,782 meters above sea level and has been continuously mined since the 16th century. Today, mining continues for other minerals like zinc and tin.
